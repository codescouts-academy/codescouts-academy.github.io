The Company

Nectios is “The best all-in-one platform for events and communities.”

Need

Nectios needed a Tech coach who could help them train their team in certain aspects, especially in good practices related to Frontend in Vue js. For this, we proposed the following 👇

Our work

  • We have collaborated with Nectios by doing Technical coaching work, accompanying both the Frontend and Backend development teams.
  • We designed a learning plan for the teams, where we focused on training and accompanying the team in their day-to-day work, noticing incredible improvements throughout the day.
  • Within the learning plan, we worked on concepts such as:
    • Mob programming
    • TDD
    • SOLID
    • Object calisthenics
    • Clean code
    • Principles of good programming
